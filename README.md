# ⚔️ SpeedMath Battle

A math game built by Aditya (age 10) with help from Claude AI.

Answer maths questions to command your army and defeat 5 waves of enemies. The better your maths, the stronger your warriors!

## How to Play

- Pick a difficulty and math type
- Tap an action button — a question appears with 4 answer choices
- Answer correctly to attack or recruit a new warrior
- Wrong answer? The enemy strikes back!
- Survive all 5 waves to win

## Warriors

| Warrior | Unlocks | Damage | Cost |
|---------|---------|--------|------|
| 🗡️ Soldier | Start | 1 | 10 💰 |
| 🏹 Archer | Wave 1 | 2 | 25 💰 |
| 🔮 Mage | Wave 2 | 4 | 45 💰 |
| 🏇 Charioteer | Wave 3 | 2 | 60 💰 |
| 🐉 Dragon Rider | Wave 4 | 7 | 90 💰 |

New warriors unlock after each wave. When your army is full, recruiting a stronger warrior automatically replaces your weakest one.
