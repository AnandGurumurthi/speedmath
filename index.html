📖 How to Play

🎯 Your Mission

Defend your castle! Defeat all 5 enemy waves before the enemies destroy your base. Each wave gets harder.

▶ How It Works

  • Your army is on the left. Enemies are on the right.
  • The red bar at the top fills up — when it is full, enemies attack automatically!
  • Click any action button to do something.
  • A maths question appears. Answer it correctly to make the action happen!
  • A wrong answer means the enemy attacks instead.

⚔️ Your Warriors

Stronger warriors deal more damage but need harder maths!

🗡️ Soldier 🟢 Easy maths · 1 damage · 4 HP 10 💰
🏹 Archer 🟡 Medium maths · 2 damage · 3 HP 25 💰
🔮 Mage 🔴 Hard maths · 4 damage · 2 HP 45 💰
🏇 Charioteer 🟠 Tough maths · 2 damage · 6 HP 60 💰
💡 Tip: The warrior at the front of your army attacks first. Put your strongest warriors in front!

To recruit a stronger warrior, you must answer MORE questions correctly in a row:

💰 Gold

You earn gold every time you answer a maths question correctly. Spend gold to recruit new warriors. You can have up to 4 warriors at once.

🧪 Healing Potions

Answer 4 questions in a row correctly to earn a healing potion! You can store up to 5 potions.

  • Tap the 🧪 button next to your castle to open potion mode
  • You have 4 seconds to choose where to use your potions
  • Tap a warrior → that warrior is fully healed!
  • Tap the 🏰 castle → restores 30% of castle HP
  • You can use multiple potions in one go if you have them!
💡 Tip: Your castle also gets a free 20% HP heal after every wave you survive!
